It is engineered to isolate the erosion of purchasing power that headline CPI — constrained by energy subsidies and broad-basket weighting — structurally misses.

Section 2 · Rationale

Why the headline inflation rate doesn't describe a Bangkok kitchen.

CPI weighting plus Price-Pack Architecture produces a widening phantom spread between official inflation and street-level survival costs. The TSSI closes that spread by measuring the exact price per gram / millilitre of a narrow, inelastic basket.

Situation

CPI is dominated by subsidized, volatile categories.

Energy and raw food drive ~90% of headline CPI movement. With state subsidies on electricity and fuel, the Ministry of Commerce's 2025 print came in at −0.14%. Core inflation stripped of those categories is still positive at +0.60% year-over-year.

Complication

FMCG manufacturers protect margin through shrinkflation.

Rather than raise nominal shelf prices, manufacturers cut net weight or volume (Price-Pack Architecture). An Ipsos survey finds 39% of Thai consumers actively notice size reductions while prices hold. Broad-basket CPI methodologies smooth over this.

Resolution

A PPU-adjusted micro-basket detects shrinkflation on arithmetic.

The TSSI tracks five standardized, factory-sealed staples at price ÷ weight. Any covert volume cut registers as a unit-price increase on the day it happens. No state subsidy can mask it.

Section 7 · Methodology

Built to be audited, not trusted.

The full conceptual framework — basket derivation, data intake, imputation, smoothing, alternative-proxy analysis, and empirical validation — lives on the methodology page.

  1. Five-asset basket

    Rice, Mama, EZYGO, Crystal water, M-150 — each a factory-sealed, COICOP-mapped Thai staple (brief §3.1).

  2. Price per unit

    PPU = nominal_price / net_weight, enforced as a database GENERATED ALWAYS column so shrinkflation registers mathematically.

  3. Imputation & smoothing

    Forward-fill capped at 3 consecutive days; beyond that, an asset is flagged structurally missing and the composite renormalizes its weight. Per-asset PPU is smoothed with a 7-day rolling median.

  4. Composite & normalization

    Weighted mean of per-asset PPU, normalized to 100 on {{ baseline_date }}. Symmetric percent change bounded in (−2, +2).
